Harvest Season Update



Here at the farm, we've been working diligently to prepare for another wonderful blueberry season and have been carefully monitoring the berries as they ripen in the summer sunshine.



If all goes well, we are hoping to begin harvesting our first variety of the season this coming weekend, beginning Saturday, June 27th. The earliest harvests often produce some of the most flavorful berries of the year and are eagerly anticipated by many of our returning friends each season.



With the warm weather we've been experiencing, the crop is looking excellent, and we are excited to once again share another season of premium spray-free blueberries with you.  If you'd like to enjoy some of the best berries this season has to offer, we encourage you to place your order early before the busiest part of the harvest season begins.

 

­

How to Order



The past year has continued to be challenging for many farms, with rising costs for packaging, fuel, equipment, labour, and other essential supplies. Despite these pressures, we remain committed to providing exceptional blueberries at a fair and affordable price.

We believe everyone should have access to fresh, high-quality blueberries at a fair and reasonable price, and we are grateful for the opportunity to continue serving our community.

Expansion News



This year marks the beginning of an exciting new chapter for our farm. As part of our ongoing growth and vision for the future, we are introducing a new name: Blueberry Blessings.



While our commitment to producing exceptional blueberries remains unchanged, the new name reflects a broader vision that is beginning to take shape.



Over the coming months, we will be sharing more information about two exciting initiatives currently in development: The Growth Project, a community-based mentorship and learning initiative for children and youth, and The Growth Project Market, a new marketplace that will connect our community with exceptional local food products, goods, services, workshops, and experiences.



These initiatives are in their early stages, but we are excited about the possibilities ahead and look forward to sharing more details with you in our next updates.

­

Get Involved in Our Community



As we begin this exciting new chapter for Blueberry Blessings, we are looking for community-minded individuals who would like to help us grow.



One of the primary goals behind Blueberry Blessings, The Growth Project (TGP), and TGP Market is to strengthen and support our local farming community. Many farmers and small producers are facing increasing challenges due to rising costs, changing economic conditions, and limited access to customers and distribution channels. Through these initiatives, we hope to create new opportunities that help local producers thrive while providing our community with greater access to exceptional local products and services.



To help bring this vision to life, we are currently looking for people with experience or interest in areas such as marketing, social media, photography, videography, graphic design, website development, communications, community outreach, and business development.



If you share our vision and would like to contribute your skills, ideas, experience, or time, we would love to hear from you. Whether you can help for a few hours or become involved on an ongoing basis, your support can make a meaningful difference.



Interested in helping? Simply reply to this email and tell us a little about yourself, your interests, and how you would like to get involved. Together, we can build something that strengthens our community and creates opportunities that benefit everyone.
